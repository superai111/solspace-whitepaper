1. Overview

Solspace is an on-chain gaming protocol built on Solana, starting with a multiplier-based game inspired by the ‚Äúcrash‚Äù genre. The project explores a community-owned game model where players, builders, and early supporters participate in shaping and growing the ecosystem through the native SOLP token.

SOLP is designed as a governance and coordination token, representing early participation in Solspace. It enables community alignment, signaling, and future incentive mechanisms once the protocol reaches sufficient maturity.

2. Vision

Traditional online games centralize ownership, control, and revenue. Players contribute time, liquidity, and attention but rarely participate in long-term value creation.

  • On-chain logic where possible
  • Transparent recording of in-game activity
  • Community-aligned incentives
  • Progressive decentralization

The long-term vision is a game protocol guided by its ecosystem rather than a single entity.

3. Why Solana

  • High throughput suitable for real-time games
  • Low transaction costs
  • Rich wallet ecosystem (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack)
  • Strong tooling for on-chain and off-chain development

4. Solspace Game (Overview)

  • Each round starts at 1.00x
  • The multiplier increases over time
  • Players may exit at any moment
  • The round ends when the multiplier crashes

Initially, some game logic may operate off-chain for performance reasons, with a roadmap toward deeper on-chain verification.
