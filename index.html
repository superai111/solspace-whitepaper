1. Overview

Solspace is an on-chain gaming protocol built on Solana, starting with a multiplier-based game inspired by the ‚Äúcrash‚Äù genre. The project explores a community-owned game model where players, builders, and early supporters participate in shaping and growing the ecosystem through the native SOLP token.

SOLP is designed as a governance and coordination token representing early participation in Solspace.

2. Vision

  • On-chain logic where possible
  • Transparent recording of in-game activity
  • Community-aligned incentives
  • Progressive decentralization

The long-term vision is a game protocol guided by its ecosystem rather than a single entity.

3. Why Solana

  • High throughput suitable for real-time games
  • Low transaction costs
  • Rich wallet ecosystem (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack)
  • Strong on-chain and off-chain tooling

4. Solspace Game (Overview)

  • Each round starts at 1.00x
  • The multiplier increases over time
  • Players may exit at any moment
  • The round ends when the multiplier crashes

Initially, some game logic may operate off-chain for performance reasons, with a roadmap toward deeper on-chain verification.

5. SOLP Token

SOLP serves as the native token of the Solspace ecosystem, enabling governance, coordination, and future incentive mechanisms.

6. Tokenomics

  • Token name: Solspace Token
  • Symbol: SOLP
  • Total supply: 1,000,000,000

7. Governance

Governance will evolve in stages, from core team execution to community signaling and eventually on-chain or off-chain voting.

8. Revenue & Sustainability

Solspace may generate revenue through game volume, platform fees, and ecosystem integrations. Allocation of revenue will be governed by the community once the protocol matures.

9. Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Token launch & community building
  • Phase 2: Alpha game release
  • Phase 3: Public beta & governance testing
  • Phase 4: Expansion & decentralization

10. Conclusion

Solspace is an experiment in community-driven on-chain gaming. SOLP represents belief in that journey.
